### How it works
1. Pick a **Track**.
2. Do each **Module** in order.
3. Check **Outcomes** and complete **Tasks**.
4. Take the **Quiz** (short).
5. Save your **Evidence** (PDFs, screenshots) in `/assets/downloads`.
